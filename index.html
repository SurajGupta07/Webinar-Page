Webinar on "Cybersecurity for Power and Energy Infrastructure"- Samgacchadhwam Series webinar 15

The electrical power infrastructure – the Electric Grid – is an extremely large cyber-physical system of national importance. Unlike other cyber-physical systems, the sheer size and geographic scale of this system, reliability concerns, and it’s status as a public utility pose some unique challenges in system security. While the system contains a lot of legacy equipment, recent trends in renewable energy deployment, electric vehicle uptake and evolution of electric loads (microgrids) pose cyber-security challenges that are yet to be understood.

Webinar on "Introduction to MITRE ATT&CK framework" - Samgacchadhwam Series webinar 14

MITRE’s ATT&CK framework has been gaining a lot of popularity in the past few years and has become the de-facto standard for building cyber-threat-detection capabilities. ATT&CK is a global repository for various techniques seen in-the-wild by the adversaries/threat actors at each stage of the cyber-attack kill chain. With MITRE’s ATT&CK, we get more than 300 cyber-attack indicators that can be leveraged to build cyber-attack detection capabilities.
